Nutrigo
Calculate nutrition for your favourite recipe!

Nutrigo is developed by Piotr Kopacki (MakuZo).

Nutrigo tries to guess and match ingredients from your recipe to products from USDA's food database.

Note that results may vary depending on the format of your recipe, ingredients that you use and weights/amounts that you provide. With that said, our calculations are not meant to be considered as real.
